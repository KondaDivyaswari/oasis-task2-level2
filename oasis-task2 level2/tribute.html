Biography

Sushant Singh Rajput (21 January 1986 – 14 June 2020) was an Indian actor for his work in Hindi cinema. He starred in a number of commercially successful Hindi films such as M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story (2016), Kedarnath (2018) and Chhichhore (2019). He received a Screen Award and was nominated for the Filmfare Awards on three occasions 1. He appeared on Forbes India’s Celebrity 100 list twice since 2017 12. For his performance in the sports biopic of Mahendra Singh Dhoni, he received his first nomination for the Filmfare Award for Best Actor On 25 September 2014, Sushant Singh Rajput was announced as the actor who would play the role of Dhoni in the biopic which was titled M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story and directed by Neeraj Pandey On 14 June 2020, Rajput, aged 34, was found dead in his home in Bandra, Mumbai.[1][184] He had reportedly shown signs of clinical depression and had bipolar disorder. Late actor Sushant Singh Rajput had a special connection with Astro-science. His curiosity to study the universe is well known. The actor had even bought an expensive telescope for space gazing. He even owned land on the moon. Sushant had also signed a film ‘Chanda Mama Door Ke for which he had undergone training at NASA. Now, to honour Sushant’s birthday anniversary, America’s Luna Society has dedicated his 2023 birth date as ‘Sushant Moon.’
